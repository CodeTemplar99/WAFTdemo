What is Halo Sport?

Halo Sport is a brain stimulator that helps you develop muscle memory faster

How Halo Sport Works

Remember when you were a kid and you could pick up skills practically overnight? Plasticity (your brain's ability to fine-tune itself) makes that possible.

As you age, your brain starts to lose plasticity, and it's takes more time to learn. Halo Sport temporarily increases plasticity in the motor cortex — so you can learn fast like a kid again.

How to use Halo Sport

Prime for 20. Practice for 60.

Wear Halo Sport for the first 20 minutes of your practice session. Then take it off and keep practicing. Your brain will stay in a state of hyperlearning for about an hour after you Prime.
