If you are unsatisfied with your purchase of a product from www.haloneuro.com for any reason, you have 30 days from the date of purchase to request a full refund. You may return any product purchased from www.haloneuro.com (a “Product”).

If you purchased Halo Sport 2 between November 14, 2019 and December 31, 2019, you can request a full refund until January 31, 2020.

To qualify for a refund, all the following conditions must be met:

· You must obtain a Return Merchandize Authorization (RMA). To request an RMA, you must request an RMA by providing the information requested on the Halo Support portal at support.haloneuro.com. If you have purchased a Silver or Gold service package from Halo, you must submit your request for an RMA to your account manager or provide the information requested on the Enterprise support portal at support.haloneuro.com;

· The returned Product(s) must be in good physical condition accompanied by the original invoice. “Good physical condition” means that the Product includes original packaging and all materials, with the Halo Sport headset in like-new condition and the corresponding primers and accessories not physically broken or damaged; and

· You must deliver the Product, along with your RMA and all accessories originally included with your purchase, in its original packaging to the address specified by Halo.

· You are responsible for and must prepay all shipping charges applicable to your return. You shall assume all risk of loss or damage to the Product while in transit to Halo.

If you return a Product to Halo which does not satisfy the above conditions, Halo retains the right to, at its sole discretion, either refuse delivery of such return or charge you a restocking fee of 15% of the original price of the product or the retail value of the missing accessories, whichever is higher.

Refunds will be processed and paid within thirty (30) days of Halo’s receipt of a returned Product satisfying the above conditions. Credit will be issued to the payment method or credit card used for your original purchase only.

For questions regarding our return policy, please email us at support@haloneuro.com.
