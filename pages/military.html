Overview

What is Halo Sport?

Halo Sport is the first headset that stimulates the part of your brain that controls movement. 20 minutes of neurostimulation induces a temporary state (~60 min) of hyperlearning. Your brain can learn the movement you practice about twice as fast if you train in this window. Watch the video to see Navy SEAL veterans put Halo Sport into action.

45% Faster Results

With identical training, Halo Sport users reached goal performance 5 days sooner compared to a control group (45% faster).

All movement is learned in the brain

Playing piano, squatting a barbell, and running a marathon look different — but your brain learns each of these movements the same way. While the pathways in the brain that produce each movement differ, they're all created through repetition and plasticity.

Enhanced multi-tasking ability

The AFRL tested Halo Sport for its application to improve multi-tasking. Stimulation with Halo Sport showed statistically significant improvement over a control group in a battery of multi-tasking tests (MATB). The improvement was consistent throughout progressive stages of difficulty.
