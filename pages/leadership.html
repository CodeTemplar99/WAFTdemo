The Halo Story

Our mission at Halo Neuroscience is to champion human potential by creating life-changing neurotechnology for all.

Before Halo, our founders spent 10+ years developing a closed-loop neurostimulator to treat epilepsy. This device, the world's first, was unanimously approved by the FDA.
